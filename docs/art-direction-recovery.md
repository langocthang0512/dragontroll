# Art direction recovery

## Visual thesis

Lost World uses premium modern pixel art rather than minimalist retro pixel art. Forms are built from crisp clustered pixels, four-to-six-step material ramps, controlled outline colors, selective warm highlights, and decorative surface breakup. Cool teal and emerald layers establish depth; coral cloth, antique gold, ivory steel, and luminous jade identify interactive objects.

## Character language

The player keeps a compact platformer collision silhouette while the rendered frame adds a scarf/cape, shoulder plate, layered tunic, belt hardware, reinforced boots, gauntlets, and a broad jeweled sword. The attack frames carry a longer blade arc and the combat hitbox is aligned to that visible reach. Male and female variants use an identical body and outfit; hair is the only variation.

## World language

- Terrain: modular carved stone, dark recesses, cracks, moss shelves, roots, and top-edge highlights.
- Dragons: three crest and palette families with scale clusters, warm eyes, animated body bob, and alternating feet.
- Gold: faceted jewel-coin interpretation with spin, bob, dark rim, hot core, and ivory glint.
- Falling eggs: aubergine shell, coral and cream facets, warning-column pulse, and telegraph wobble.
- Spikes: pale metal points mounted in red sockets over carved dark stone.
- Checkpoint and goal: layered masonry, living vines, luminous glyphs, and restrained animated glow.

## Source studies

The project includes two original internal direction studies generated with the built-in image generation tool:

- `assets/temp/visual-recovery-direction.webp`
- `assets/temp/visual-recovery-character-study.webp`

The shipped background panorama is `assets/environment/lost-world-panorama.webp`. It is an original generated Lost World environment compressed to a 1,010x540 high-quality WebP for a small browser payload, then layered beneath code-rendered platforms and interactive objects.

Direction-sheet prompt: an original modern detailed pixel-art platformer sheet with matching-body male/female adventurers, ceremonial sword, three dragon variants, faceted egg, spike trap, jewel coin, moss checkpoint door, and carved terrain; cool emerald shadows with warm gold rim light; no copied assets, text, logos, or layouts.

Panorama prompt: an original wide modern pixel-art Lost World jungle-ruin background with layered canopy, giant roots, turquoise temples, waterfalls, mist, vines, and warm light shafts; background only, quiet lower gameplay band, no characters, creatures, gameplay objects, text, logos, or copied layouts.

The supplied Dragon Mania Legends and platformer references were used only to study shape hierarchy, material richness, color grouping, and readability. No source artwork was imported or reproduced.
